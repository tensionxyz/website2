About

Deep research. Structural conviction.

Modern Heritage Capital is a digital asset investment firm built on deep fundamental research, on-chain intelligence, and long-conviction positioning. We invest in protocols and digital assets where our proprietary research identifies structural mispricings — then we hold with conviction through volatility.

We believe the most durable edge in digital assets comes not from speed, but from depth. Our research infrastructure maps the full lifecycle of a protocol — its supply dynamics, governance evolution, ecosystem growth, and the behavior of its most consequential stakeholders — to develop investment theses that compound over quarters and years, not days.

Investment Philosophy

Three principles. One process.

Understand supply before price.

Most market participants focus on demand narratives — adoption curves, user growth, partnership announcements. We start with supply. Who holds the tokens? What are their incentives? When can they sell? What structural forces — vesting schedules, staking lockups, exchange reserve dynamics — constrain or release supply into the market? Price is where supply meets demand. If you understand supply deeply, you see opportunities others miss.

Conviction requires infrastructure.

Opinions are cheap. Conviction — the kind that lets you size meaningfully and hold through drawdowns — requires a research process that generates repeatable, verifiable insights. We've built proprietary systems for on-chain entity attribution, governance monitoring, supply modeling, and ecosystem intelligence that let us move from "this seems interesting" to "we know exactly why this is mispriced and when the market will recognize it."

Asymmetry over frequency.

We don't need to be right often. We need to be right when it matters — when the payoff is asymmetric, the thesis is well-supported, and our positioning gives us time to let the investment work. We concentrate capital in our highest-conviction opportunities and manage risk through depth of understanding, not diversification for its own sake.

Research Methodology

Comprehensive understanding before capital is deployed.

01

Fundamental Analysis

We start with the basics done rigorously: What problem does this protocol solve? Who are the competitors? Who invested and what is their track record? What are the growth metrics — network activity, developer engagement, revenue, and user retention? We evaluate these through the lens of long-term viability, not short-term momentum.

02

Supply Dynamics

This is where our process diverges from most investors. We build detailed supply models for every asset we cover: total supply, circulating supply, staked and locked supply, floating supply, emission schedules, vesting cliffs, and concentration analysis across the largest holders. We layer probabilistic assessments — accounting for lock-up durations, staking exit penalties, and historical holder behavior — to estimate true liquid supply.

03

On-Chain Intelligence

We maintain a proprietary database mapping blockchain addresses to known entities — foundations, early investors, ecosystem funds, market participants, and protocol treasuries. This attribution layer transforms raw blockchain data into actionable intelligence. When a significant holder changes their behavior, we see it — and we understand the context behind it.

04

Governance & Ecosystem Monitoring

Protocol governance increasingly drives tokenomics: staking parameters, burn mechanisms, treasury deployments, supply policy changes. We monitor governance forums and on-chain proposals across our coverage universe, tracking how protocol evolution affects the investment thesis. Governance is where long-term value creation and destruction happens — most investors ignore it until it's too late.
